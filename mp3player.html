Mp3player

A MP3 player that lets users add and organize songs into a custom playlist. Users can browse and load songs from their device, view them in a list, and play, pause, or delete tracks directly within the app — providing a clean, easy-to-use interface for managing personal music collections. The project is purely based on java, javaFX for GUI design.
